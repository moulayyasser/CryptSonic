Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Basic and Pro plans. No credit card required.

What happens if I exceed my monthly encryption limit?

For Basic plans, additional encryptions are billed at $0.10 per encryption. Pro and Enterprise plans have unlimited encryptions.
